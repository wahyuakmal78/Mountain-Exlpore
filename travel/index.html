Welcome to Indonesia

Mountains in Indonesia are almost on every island in Indonesia. In addition to the mountains carrying the potential for great natural wealth (read: Indonesia's natural wealth), the mountains also provide a very beautiful view. This is what causes the mountains are often used as tourist attractions for people who live in cities and want to feel the cool nature of Indonesia. Not surprisingly, this mountainous area is often flooded with tourists, both in the holiday season and every weekend arrives.
